Mix real paint. Pick real colors.

This is not a gradient picker. It is a pigment-diffusion simulation.

  1. Pick a paint from the left rail.
  2. Drag on the canvas to drop pigment. Colors blend by real diffusion.
  3. Click any spot to sample — it auto-saves to your palette and copies the hex.
  4. Hover a swatch to see its hex; click × to remove.
